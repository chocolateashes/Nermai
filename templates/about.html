About this Project

The name of this application is Nermai, which means integrity in Tamil.This application allows users to record their experiences with government officials, both good and bad, and access the data inputted both by themselves and others in order to make better-informed decisions when they must seek administrative government services. The idea is for this application to both encourage administrative officials to stay honest and to encourage to make a difference in their own government. This application runs because of you, the citizens, and as such is run by the people, for the people because we deserve honesty and integrity from our government.
